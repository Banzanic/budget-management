Welcome to Budget Management Application

This application is designed to help you manage your finances effectively. It allows you to track and record your monthly income and expenses over the past 14 years, providing a comprehensive view of your financial health.

Key features include:

  • Income & Expense Tracking: Record your income and expenses on a monthly basis, offering insights into your spending habits and financial patterns.
  • Financial Visualization: Visualize your income and expenses over time, highlighting trends and areas for improvement.
  • Savings Goals: Set personal savings goals and monitor your progress towards achieving them, encouraging disciplined saving habits.

By leveraging our application, you can gain a clearer understanding of your financial behavior, make informed decisions, and work towards achieving your financial goals.
